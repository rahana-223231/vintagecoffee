About-Us

Vintage Coffee which was launched in 2022 in India is eyeing to capture a segment that has grown up to coffee culture.
VC is premium coffee since 2000.
At The Vinatge Coffee, the art of crafting the best tasting coffee requires respecting each individual roast.
Knowing the specific origin and roast degree for each coffee allows for intense flavor that can be traced back to its earliest beginnings.
Now VC is migrating to the next level of coffee as an experience.
The expansion for VC is along four formats including high street, property inside a mall, kiosk model and high profile office building.
